1. Overview

This privacy policy describes how the Saudi Exchange MCP connector, operated by Digitrends, handles information when accessed through Claude. We are committed to being transparent about our minimal data practices.

✅ Short version: We don't collect, log, or store any personal data. Our server is stateless — it receives a request, fetches stock data, returns it, and forgets everything.

2. What We Collect

We collect nothing. Specifically, the following are not collected, stored, or logged:

  • Your name, email, or any personal identifiers
  • Your stock queries or search history
  • Your IP address or device information
  • Authentication tokens (validated in real-time and discarded)
  • Usage patterns or behavioral data

3. What We Access Per Request

For each request made through Claude, the following data passes through our server momentarily to fulfill the request and is immediately discarded:

DataPurposeStored?
Stock symbol or company nameTo fetch the requested market dataNo
Bearer token (Auth0 JWT)To verify you are authenticatedNo
Request parametersTo return the correct data (e.g. time period)No

4. Third-Party Services

Yahoo Finance — Stock market data is retrieved from Yahoo Finance in real-time. Only the stock ticker symbol is sent in the query. No user data is ever shared with Yahoo Finance.

Auth0 — Authentication is managed by Auth0. When you log in to connect this MCP, Auth0 handles your credentials. We never see or store your password. Please refer to Auth0's Privacy Policy for details on how they manage authentication data.

5. Data Storage & Infrastructure

Our MCP server is entirely stateless. There is no database attached to this service. No data is written to disk beyond what the operating system requires to process the current HTTP request. When the request is complete, no trace remains on our servers.

The server is hosted on a secure VPS in the EU with HTTPS/TLS encryption enforced on all connections.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not store any personal data, there is no personal data held by us to access, rectify, or erase. If you have concerns about data held by Auth0 (your login account), you can contact Auth0 directly or request account deletion through their platform.

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have rights under GDPR. As we process no personal data, GDPR obligations on our part are minimal, but we remain committed to full compliance.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The latest version will always be available at this URL. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
