Saudi Exchange MCP

Real-time stock market data for Saudi Exchange (Tadawul) listed companies, accessible directly through Claude.

What It Does

This connector gives Claude access to live stock data from the Saudi Exchange (Tadawul), including prices, historical data, and company information for all major listed companies.

How to Connect

  1. Open Claude.ai
  2. Go to Settings → Connectors
  3. Find Saudi Exchange and click Connect
  4. Log in with your account when prompted
  5. You're ready — just ask Claude about Saudi stocks

Available Tools

get_stock_price

Get the real-time price for any Tadawul-listed stock.

"What is Saudi Aramco's stock price right now?"
"Get me the current price of 2222.SR"
get_top_saudi_stocks

Get live prices for the top 10 Saudi Exchange listed companies.

"Show me today's top Saudi stocks"
"Which Saudi stocks are up today?"
get_stock_history

Get historical price data for any Saudi stock. Supports periods: 1d, 5d, 1mo, 3mo, 6mo, 1y.

"Show me SABIC's stock price for the last 3 months"
"What was Al Rajhi Bank's price trend over the past year?"
get_stock_info

Get detailed company information including sector, market cap, employees, and description.

"Tell me about Saudi Aramco as a company"
"What sector is STC in and what's their market cap?"

Stock Symbol Format

Saudi Exchange stocks use the .SR suffix. Common symbols:

2222.SR — Saudi Aramco
7010.SR — STC
2010.SR — SABIC
1180.SR — Al Rajhi Bank
1211.SR — Maaden
3010.SR — Saudi Cement
