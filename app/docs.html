Saudi Exchange

Real-time stock market data for Saudi Exchange (Tadawul) listed companies — powered by Digitrends

📋 Overview

The Saudi Exchange MCP connector gives Claude direct access to live market data from the Saudi Exchange (Tadawul) — the largest stock exchange in the Middle East. Ask Claude about stock prices, company financials, historical performance, and more in natural language, without leaving your conversation.

Data is sourced in real-time from Yahoo Finance and covers all major Tadawul-listed companies including Saudi Aramco, STC, SABIC, Al Rajhi Bank, and hundreds more.

🚀 How to Connect

  1. Open Claude.ai in your browser
  2. Navigate to Settings → Connectors
  3. Find Saudi Exchange and click Connect
  4. Log in with your account when the Auth0 prompt appears
  5. You're ready — just ask Claude about Saudi stocks in any conversation

⚡ No API key or subscription required. A free account login is all you need to get started.

🛠 Available Tools

get_stock_price

Get the real-time price, change, and volume for any Tadawul-listed stock.

"What is Saudi Aramco's stock price right now?"
"Is Al Rajhi Bank up or down today?"
get_top_saudi_stocks

Get a live snapshot of the top 10 Saudi Exchange listed companies with price trends.

"Show me today's top Saudi stocks"
"Which Saudi stocks are performing best today?"
get_stock_history

Fetch historical OHLCV data for any Saudi stock. Supports 1d, 5d, 1mo, 3mo, 6mo, 1y periods.

"Show SABIC's price chart for the last 3 months"
"How has Aramco performed over the past year?"
get_stock_info

Get detailed company information — sector, market cap, employees, business description, and website.

"Tell me about STC as a company"
"What is Maaden's market cap and sector?"
